about us

Our Story

Empowering education through innovation and inclusivity

We are final-year software engineering students from SLTC University, dedicated to creating accessible learning solutions for visually impaired students in Sri Lanka.

Innovative Voice Recognition System

Our Sinhala voice-controlled audiobook system ensures seamless navigation and accessibility for visually impaired learners.

Customized Accessibility Tools

We integrate features like biometric authentication and digital overlays to support diverse user needs effectively.

more about us

our approach

Empowering access to knowledge and stories

our mission

To create an inclusive platform that provides easy access to audiobooks, empowering individuals—especially the visually impaired—to explore literature, education, and entertainment through sound.

our vision

To be a leading digital library that bridges the gap in accessibility by offering a rich collection of Sinhala and multilingual audiobooks for lifelong learning and inspiration.

our goals

To expand audiobook content, collaborate with publishers and volunteers, and implement user-friendly voice-based navigation systems to serve communities across Sri Lanka and beyond.

what we do

Transforming Access to Education Through Voice

We design innovative tools for visually impaired students to engage with university content effortlessly. Our Sinhala voice-controlled audiobook system bridges accessibility gaps in higher education. By integrating smart tech and inclusive design, we empower every learner to thrive.

  • Extensive Digital Catalog
  • Interactive Learning Tools
  • Personalized Book Suggestions
  • Accessible for All Users
